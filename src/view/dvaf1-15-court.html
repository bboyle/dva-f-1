{{RespondentName true}} will be given a copy of the application which includes a notice to appear in court at the same time.

Courthouses have safety measures in place such as safe rooms (or other rooms or areas where {{theAggrievedYou}} can wait separately). Most courthouses also have security officers and security cameras.

Court staff follow domestic violence protocols which place safety as top priority for all people seeking protection from domestic violence.

    The court staff will copy the form and give it to the security officer, domestic violence protection worker, registrar and any other relevant court staff to arrange for your safety. It will also be placed on your court file, but will not be part of the application.
